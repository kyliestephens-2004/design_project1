# Press Release

# Header: AI Model Predicts Hospital Readmission Risk to Improve Patient Care

## Hook: What if hospitals could identify patients most likely to return before they even leave?

## Problem Statement
Hospital readmissions are a major challenge for healthcare systems worldwide. Many patients return within 30 days after discharge due to complications, incomplete recovery, or lack of follow-up care. These readmissions not only negatively impact patient health but also increase costs and strain hospital resources. Hospitals are often penalized financially for high readmission rates, creating an urgent need to proactively identify patients at risk and implement preventive care measures.

## Solution Description
This project uses a predictive analytics model to estimate the likelihood of hospital readmission for each patient based on historical data, including demographics, medical history, diagnoses, and prior admissions. Healthcare providers can use these predictions to intervene early with personalized care plans, schedule follow-ups, or provide additional support for high-risk patients, ultimately improving patient outcomes and reducing unnecessary hospital visits. Implementing a predictive model like this into healthcare will benefit not only healthcare workers and providers, but also patients and insurance companies, effectively saving money, time, and lives.
